# 🔤 TF-IDF

<br><br>

## Definition

TF-IDF stands for:

- Term Frequency  
- Inverse Document Frequency  

<br><br>

## Objective

To identify relevant terms in large text collections.

<br><br>

## Formula

TF-IDF = TF × IDF  

<br><br>

## Application in this Project

The pipeline uses TF-IDF to:

- identify keywords  
- detect financial trends  
- prioritize relevant terms  
- power dashboards  

<br><br>

## Example Terms

- dividend  
- passive income  
- real estate investment fund (REIT/FII)  
- liquidity
